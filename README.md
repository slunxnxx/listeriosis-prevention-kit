# Listeriosis Prevention Educational Resource Kit

## Purpose
This open-source educational resource kit is designed to provide comprehensive, evidence-based information on listeriosis prevention for public health organizations, community groups, retailers, and consumers.

## Intended Audience
- Public health departments and agencies
- Community health educators
- Retail food safety teams
- High-risk populations (pregnant women, elderly, immunocompromised individuals)
- General consumers seeking food safety guidance

## Contents
The kit currently includes:
- **Consumer Fact Sheet**: Printable handout with key prevention strategies, high-risk foods to avoid, and safe food handling practices.
- **Home Safety Checklist**: Concise guide for safe food handling at home (coming soon).

## Goals
1. Reduce the incidence of preventable listeriosis cases through public education.
2. Increase awareness of *Listeria monocytogenes* and its transmission routes.
3. Promote compliance with food safety regulations and best practices.
4. Provide accessible, ready-to-use materials for widespread distribution.
